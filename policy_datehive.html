VibeMatch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025 · Effective for the VibeMatch mobile app

VibeMatch ("we", "us", or "our") provides a dating application to help adults meet and connect. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

  • Email, encrypted password, date of birth, display name
  • Profile information (photos, bio, interests)
  • Messages and chats
  • Payment transaction IDs (Paystack — no card data stored)
  • Device diagnostics and push notification tokens (Firebase)

2. How we use your data

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Dating, matching, messaging features
  • Subscription and payment management
  • Security, fraud prevention, abuse monitoring

3. Data sharing

  • Supabase (database & storage)
  • Firebase (push notifications)
  • Paystack (payments)
  • Law enforcement if legally required

4. Children

VibeMatch is strictly for adults **18+ only**. We do not knowingly collect data from minors and immediately delete any underage account found.

5. User rights

You can request deletion of your account directly in the app or via email.
