DateHive ("we", "us", or "our") provides a dating application to help adults meet and connect. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and your choices.

1. What information we collect

We collect the following categories of information from users who create an account or interact with the app.

Account & profile data

  • Required: Email address, encrypted password (for authentication), date of birth, display name.
  • Optional profile info: Gender, bio, interests, education, photos you upload, and other profile fields you choose to provide.

Communications

  • Messages you send to other users (stored to enable chat).
  • Support messages and feedback you submit to us.

Payment & billing

  • We do not store full payment credentials. Payment processing is handled by Paystack. We store transaction identifiers and subscription status needed to manage your paid features.

Device & technical data

  • Device type, operating system version, app version, and crash and error logs (for diagnostics).
  • IP address and timestamps for account activity and security monitoring.

Push notifications

  • We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications. We store device tokens necessary to deliver notifications.

Storage & hosting

  • Profile data, messages, and user-uploaded images are stored in your project's backend managed through Supabase (database + storage).
Important: We do not collect or use GPS / precise device location. If you enable device-level location services outside the app, DateHive does not access that location data.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To enable messaging and matching features inside the app.
  • To process payments and manage subscription status via Paystack.
  • To send transactional and service notifications (using Firebase Cloud Messaging).
  • To maintain, improve, and secure the service (diagnostics, fraud prevention, abuse detection).
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Legal bases (for users in the EEA / GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our service (account creation, chat, subscriptions).
  • Consent: Where required (e.g., optional features you enable).
  • Legitimate interests: For fraud prevention, security, and improving our service (balanced against your rights).

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Paystack (payment processing), Supabase (backend storage/database), Firebase (push notifications and optional analytics).
  • Other users: The profile information you choose to make visible (display name, age, photos, bio) is visible to other users and will be shared as part of normal app features (matches, messages).
  • Legal requests: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.
Note: If you connect any third-party accounts (for example, third-party social logins), the information shared will depend on the permissions you grant to those services.

5. How long we retain data

We retain your personal data for as long as your account exists and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you delete your account we will remove your personal data from active systems within a reasonable period, subject to legal or operational retention requirements (for example, transaction records).
